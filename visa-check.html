Your passport — is it valid at least 6 months beyond your return date?

This is the single most common reason applications get bounced at the counter.

Does it have at least 2 blank visa pages?

Embassies need physical space for the visa sticker and entry stamps.

Your travel history in the last 5 years?

Prior visas signal you travel and return. Pick the strongest that applies.

Ever had a visa refused or an overstay flagged?

Be honest here — refusals aren't fatal, but they change how you should apply.

Your business standing?

Business visas hinge on proving you're a genuine trader with reason to return.

Do you have an official invitation letter from the expo organiser?

For China especially, this is non-negotiable for a business (M) visa.

Can you show 6 months of bank statements with healthy activity?

Personal or business account showing you can fund the trip.

When do you plan to submit your application?

Embassy processing from Nairobi typically runs 1–4 weeks — longer in peak season.
