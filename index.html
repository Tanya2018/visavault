Three lies that cost Kenyans millions

These are free. The full per-country criteria are inside the vault.

"You need an ETIAS for Europe"

False for Kenyan passports. ETIAS is only for visa-exempt nationals. You need a full Schengen Type C visa. Agents sell fake "ETIAS help" all the time.

"I can guarantee your US visa"

Nobody can. The consular officer decides, alone, at interview. Anyone promising approval for a fee is running a scam — full stop.

"Pay me to book your appointment"

You can book your own VFS / embassy slot for free. Paying a "fixer" to click a calendar is pure markup on a task you can do yourself.

"Here's a Certificate of Sponsorship / LMIA / job offer"

Bought sponsorship documents are fraud — and they ban you. Real work routes exist; the shortcut ones are traps.
